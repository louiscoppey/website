About

Hi, I am Louis. I studied math and computer science and I have been a Venture investor at Poine Nine for about 10 years. Most of my work is to study, invest in and work with software companies.

My current areas of interest include AI and Data Infrastructure, Vertical Software, B2B marketplaces and software that play a role in a more ecological and inclusive future.

I believe my role is to channel capital in businesses that move the world in the right direction.
